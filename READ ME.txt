HOLD THE FREQUENCY
Chrysasynth — Standalone Edition

Thank you for downloading this tool.

Hold the Frequency is a minimal sonic experiment
from the Chrysasynth lab.

-----------------------------------

HOW TO USE

1. Extract the folder
2. Open "index.html" in your browser
3. Click or touch the interface to start audio
4. Hold the frequency and explore the sound

-----------------------------------

COMPATIBILITY

Works in modern browsers:
• Chrome
• Edge
• Safari
• Firefox

Desktop and mobile supported.

Note: audio starts after user interaction due to browser audio policies.
